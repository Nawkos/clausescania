## 2. Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Access the Application

Visit: **https://clausescania.lovable.app**

Works on desktop, mobile, and tablets with any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

### Step 2: Upload Your Contract

**Supported Formats:**

| Format | Extension | Max Size |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| PDF    | .pdf      | 10 MB    |
| Word   | .docx     | 10 MB    |
| Text   | .txt      | 10 MB    |

**Upload Methods:**
- Drag and drop file to upload zone
- Click "Choose File" to browse files
- Mobile: Tap to select from device

**Requirements:**
- File must not be password-protected
- Text must be readable (not scanned images)
- Ensure stable internet connection

### Step 3: Wait for Analysis

Analysis takes approximately 30 seconds:
1. Document parsing (2-5 seconds)
2. AI clause detection (15-20 seconds)
3. Risk pattern analysis (5-10 seconds)
4. Report generation (2-5 seconds)

**Note:** Do not close or refresh the page during analysis.

### Step 4: Review Your Results

After analysis, you'll see:
- Risk score (0-100) with color coding
- Alert categories (Critical, Moderate, Attention)
- Specific recommendations for each issue
- Option to export PDF report

## 3. Understanding Results

### Risk Score Explained

| Score Range | Color | Meaning | Recommended Action |
|-------------|-------|---------|-------------------|
| 71-100 | Green | Safe | Proceed with confidence, review minor points |
| 41-70 | Orange | Caution | Review alerts carefully, negotiate concerns |
| 0-40 | Red | Danger | Do not sign, seek legal advice, major changes needed |

### Alert Levels

**Critical Alerts (Red)**
- Severity: High
- Impact: Deal-breakers with significant financial or legal risk
- Examples: Loss of IP rights, excessive penalties, illegal clauses
- Action: Must be negotiated or removed before signing

**Moderate Alerts (Orange)**
- Severity: Medium
- Impact: Significant concerns that should be addressed
- Examples: Unbalanced terms, long notice periods, missing protections
- Action: Strongly recommended to negotiate

**Attention Points (Yellow)**
- Severity: Low
- Impact: Minor issues or unclear language
- Examples: Ambiguous wording, slightly unfavorable terms
- Action: Good to clarify, but not critical

### Alert Card Information

Each alert shows:

1. **Header:** Severity badge, clause reference, page number, title
2. **Clause Excerpt:** Quote of problematic text
3. **Problem:** Clear explanation of the concern
4. **Recommendation:** Specific negotiation advice
5. **Location:** Exact page and line reference

### Market Comparison

Results include industry benchmarking:
- Your score vs. industry average
- Percentile ranking against similar contracts
- Best score comparison

**Interpreting Percentile:**
- Below 50%: Better than most contracts
- 50-70%: Average contract
- Above 70%: Worse than most contracts (caution)

### Missing Clauses

The analysis identifies important protections not included in your contract, such as:
- Force majeure protection
- Dispute resolution process
- Payment terms specification
- Mutual confidentiality

**Action:** Request these clauses be added to protect both parties.

### Export Options

**PDF Report:**
- Click "Export Report" button
- Includes risk score, findings, recommendations, and highlights

**Save Contract:**
- Click "Save Contract" to store in browser

**View Highlighted Document:**

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What file formats are supported?

PDF (.pdf), Word (.docx), and Text (.txt) files up to 10MB. Files must not be password-protected.

### 2. How long does analysis take?

Typically 20-40 seconds depending on contract length and complexity.

### 3. Is my contract data stored?

No. Contracts are processed temporarily in-memory and never stored permanently unless you explicitly save them in your browser.

### 4. How accurate is the AI analysis?

The AI has been tested against multiple contract scenarios with high accuracy. However, it is not a substitute for professional legal advice.

### 5. Can I export the results?

Yes. Click "Export Report" to download a professional PDF with all findings and recommendations.

### 6. What types of contracts work best?

Employment contracts, freelance agreements, SaaS subscriptions, rental leases, NDAs, service contracts, and partnership agreements.

### 7. Is this legal advice?

No. ClauseScan AI provides automated analysis for informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified attorney before signing contracts.

### 8. What if my file is too large?

Maximum file size is 10MB. If your file exceeds this, try converting to TXT format or compressing the PDF.

### 9. Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The application is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

### 10. How much does it cost?

ClauseScan AI is free to use for contract analysis. No account or payment required.
