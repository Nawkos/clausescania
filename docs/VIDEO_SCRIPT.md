## üé® Visual Guidelines

### Screen Recording
- **Clean browser:** No bookmarks bar, extensions, or personal info
- **Full screen:** Press F11 before recording
- **Smooth scrolling:** Scroll slowly and smoothly
- **Purposeful clicks:** Click deliberately, not frantically
- **Pauses:** Hold on important information for 2-3 seconds

### Cursor Movement
- **Slow and smooth:** Don't dart around the screen
- **Highlight:** Use cursor to draw attention to important elements
- **Click feedback:** OBS can add click animations

### Pacing
- **Don't rush:** Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- **Match audio to visual:** Time voiceover with screen actions
- **Breathing room:** Pause briefly between sections

---

## üé§ Voiceover Tips

### Recording Quality
- **Microphone:** Use headset or external mic (not laptop mic)
- **Environment:** Quiet room, no background noise
- **Distance:** 6-8 inches from microphone
- **Pop filter:** Reduce "p" and "b" sounds

### Speaking Style
- **Pace:** Moderate speed (not too fast, not too slow)
- **Tone:** Professional but friendly
- **Energy:** Enthusiastic without being overly dramatic
- **Clarity:** Enunciate clearly, especially technical terms

### Script Delivery
- **Practice:** Read script 3-5 times before recording
- **Natural:** Don't sound like you're reading
- **Emphasis:** Stress important points (e.g., "30 seconds," "94% accuracy")
- **Pauses:** Brief pauses between sentences for clarity
