FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Freelance Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on November 2, 2025, between:

CLIENT: TechCorp Solutions Inc.
Address: 123 Business Street, Paris, France
("Client")

AND

CONTRACTOR: Jean Dupont
Address: 45 Rue de la Paix, Paris, France
("Contractor")

1. SCOPE OF WORK
The Contractor agrees to provide web development services as specified by the Client from time to time. The scope may be modified at the Client's sole discretion without additional compensation.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1 The Client agrees to pay €5,000 per month for services rendered.
2.2 Payment will be made within 90 days of invoice receipt.
2.3 Late payment penalties do not apply to the Client.
2.4 The Contractor may not dispute invoices after 5 days of issuance.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS
3.1 All work product, including but not limited to code, designs, documentation, ideas, concepts, and any derivative works, shall become the exclusive property of the Client immediately upon creation.
3.2 The Contractor hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in perpetuity.
3.3 The Contractor waives all moral rights and attribution rights.
3.4 The Contractor may not use any work product created for portfolio purposes without written consent.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
4.1 The Contractor agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all Client information.
4.2 This obligation survives termination indefinitely.
4.3 The Client has no reciprocal confidentiality obligations.

5. TERM AND TERMINATION
5.1 This Agreement shall remain in effect indefinitely until terminated.
5.2 The Client may terminate this Agreement at any time without notice or cause.
5.3 The Contractor must provide 90 days written notice to terminate.
5.4 Upon termination by the Client, no compensation is owed for work in progress.

6. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
6.1 During the term and for 5 years after termination, the Contractor shall not:
    a) Provide services to any company in the technology sector
    b) Work with any current or former clients of the Client
    c) Solicit any employees, contractors, or clients of the Client
6.2 This restriction applies globally without geographical limitation.

7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
7.1 The Contractor assumes all liability for any defects, errors, or issues in deliverables.
7.2 The Contractor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client from any and all claims, including legal fees.
7.3 There is no limitation on the Contractor's liability.
7.4 The Client's liability is limited to €100 for any and all claims.

8. PENALTIES
8.1 Late delivery of any milestone results in automatic penalties of €1,000 per day.
8.2 Penalties begin immediately without grace period or notice.
8.3 Quality issues subject to additional €500 per incident as determined solely by Client.

9. WORK REQUIREMENTS
9.1 The Contractor must be available 24/7 for urgent requests.
9.2 Response time must not exceed 1 hour at any time, including weekends and holidays.
9.3 The Contractor may not take vacation without 60 days advance notice and Client approval.

10. MODIFICATIONS
10.1 The Client may modify this Agreement at any time by providing written notice.
10.2 Continued work after notification constitutes acceptance of modifications.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
11.1 Any disputes shall be resolved exclusively in the courts of Delaware, USA.
11.2 The Contractor waives the right to jury trial.
11.3 The prevailing party in any dispute shall be entitled to attorney's fees from the non-prevailing party.

12. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
12.1 The Contractor represents that all work will be perfect, error-free, and meet unstated Client expectations.
12.2 The Contractor provides unlimited warranty on all work in perpetuity.

13. INSURANCE
13.1 The Contractor must maintain professional liability insurance with minimum coverage of €5,000,000.
13.2 Proof of insurance must be provided upon request.

14. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
14.1 The Contractor is an independent contractor but must work exclusively for the Client.
14.2 The Contractor may not engage in any other business activities without prior written consent.

15. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements.
